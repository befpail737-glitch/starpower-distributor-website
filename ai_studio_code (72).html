FAE Application Insights

The GD25SLS120C6S integrates a full three-phase inverter into a single, compact module. This is the ideal solution for creating extremely power-dense motor drives, such as servo controllers and small general-purpose drives up to 7.5kW. It significantly reduces assembly complexity and saves valuable board space compared to using three separate half-bridge modules.
