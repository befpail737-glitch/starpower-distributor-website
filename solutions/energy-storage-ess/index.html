Bidirectional Converter Solution for Energy Storage Systems (ESS)

High-Reliability Design for Grid-Tied Applications

Home » Solutions » ESS Solution

Energy Storage Systems (ESS) are critical to modernizing the electrical grid. The heart of any ESS is the Power Conversion System (PCS), which must efficiently manage bidirectional power flow. This solution outlines a robust PCS design using Starpower power modules, ensuring high efficiency and long-term reliability for grid-tied battery storage.

Key Advantages

  • Bidirectional Efficiency: Low-loss Starpower IGBTs are efficient in both charging (rectifier) and discharging (inverter) modes.
  • Grid-Tied Reliability: Modules are designed for high power cycling capability, essential for grid-support applications like frequency regulation.
  • Scalable Architecture: The design can be paralleled to create multi-megawatt storage systems.
