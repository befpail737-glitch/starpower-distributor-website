High-Efficiency Modular UPS Solution

A Scalable Design for Critical Power Applications

Home » Solutions » Modular UPS Solution

Modern data centers and critical facilities demand Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) that are not only reliable but also highly efficient and scalable. This solution outlines a modular UPS design leveraging Starpower IGBT modules to achieve over 96% double-conversion efficiency, reducing operating costs and environmental impact.

Solution Block Diagram

The design features a high-frequency PFC rectifier and a 3-phase inverter.

Key Advantages

  • High Efficiency: Starpower's low-loss IGBTs minimize both conduction and switching losses in the PFC and inverter stages.
  • Scalability: The design is based on hot-swappable power modules (e.g., 25kW, 50kW), allowing for easy N+1 redundancy and pay-as-you-grow capacity expansion.
  • High Power Factor: The IGBT-based PFC rectifier ensures a near-unity input power factor (>0.99), reducing stress on the utility grid.
  • Compact Design: High switching frequencies enable the use of smaller magnetic components, leading to higher power density.
