800V EV Main Inverter Solution featuring Starpower SiC Modules

A technical overview of a reference design for next-generation electric vehicles.

Home » Solutions » EV Main Inverter

The shift to 800V architectures in electric vehicles demands power modules with lower switching losses, higher thermal performance, and supreme reliability. This reference solution leverages Starpower's automotive-grade Silicon Carbide (SiC) modules to achieve industry-leading efficiency and power density.

Key Advantages

  • High Efficiency: Starpower SiC MOSFETs dramatically reduce switching losses, leading to over 99% inverter efficiency and increased vehicle range.
  • High Power Density: Superior thermal performance and lower losses allow for a smaller heatsink and a more compact, lightweight inverter design.
  • Faster Charging: The 800V system enables ultra-fast DC charging capabilities.
  • Automotive-Grade Reliability: Built with modules that are fully qualified for demanding automotive environments.
