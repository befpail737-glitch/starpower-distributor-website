Compact Power Stage Solution for Industrial Servo Drives

Featuring Starpower PIM G7 Series Intelligent Power Modules (IPM)

Home » Solutions » Industrial Servo Drive

In the competitive field of industrial automation, servo drives demand high precision, compact size, and robust reliability. This reference solution simplifies the design of the power inverter stage by using a Starpower Intelligent Power Module (IPM), integrating the power switches, gate drivers, and protection circuits into a single package.

Solution Block Diagram

The IPM simplifies the design by containing the entire 3-phase inverter stage.

Key Advantages

  • High Integration: The Starpower PIM G7 IPM includes the IGBTs, FWDs, gate drivers, and protection features (UVLO, OCP, OTP), drastically reducing component count and PCB size.
  • Simplified Design: Eliminates the complexity of designing, sourcing, and qualifying a discrete gate driver circuit.
  • Enhanced Reliability: The integrated and optimized gate drive ensures reliable switching and protects the IGBTs from fault conditions.
  • Excellent Thermal Performance: Offered in industry-standard packages for easy mounting to a heatsink.
