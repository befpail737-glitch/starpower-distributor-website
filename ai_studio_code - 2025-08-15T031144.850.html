FAE Application Insights

The GD75FAT120C2S is designed for the Active Neutral-Point Clamped (ANPC) topology, an advanced version of the 3-level NPC inverter. ANPC topologies offer even lower switching losses and more balanced thermal distribution among the semiconductor dies. This makes the module a premium choice for engineers developing next-generation, ultra-high efficiency (>99%) solar inverters and online UPS systems.
