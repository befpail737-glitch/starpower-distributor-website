FAE Application Insights

The GD600FCT170C2S provides a single high-power switch in a module, often called a "chopper" configuration. This is ideal for high-power DC-DC converters, such as the boost converter in a solar MPPT stage or the buck/boost converter in a battery charging system. It is also commonly used as the braking chopper in industrial motor drive applications to dissipate regenerative energy.
