FAE Application Insights

The 'P' in the part number indicates Press-Fit pins. This module is designed for automated, solder-free assembly processes. The press-fit connection creates a highly reliable, gas-tight electrical and mechanical bond to the PCB. This technology is preferred in high-volume industrial and automotive manufacturing where process consistency and the elimination of solder joint failures are critical for long-term reliability.
