Starpower IPM - GD30PJM120C1S

1200V, 30A Intelligent Power Module for Appliance Motor Control

Key Features:

  • Highly Integrated: Includes 3-phase inverter bridge, gate drivers, and protection.
  • Built-in Protection: Under-voltage lockout (UVLO), short-circuit protection (OCP), over-temperature (OTP).
  • Optimized for Low Noise: Soft switching characteristics for quiet motor operation.
  • Single Power Supply Drive Circuit
  • Directly connects to MCU/DSP control signals.
