Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a 1700V rating needed for a 1500VDC system?

A safety margin is required to handle potential voltage overshoots during switching and fluctuations in the DC bus voltage. A 1700V-rated device provides the necessary robustness and reliability for a nominal 1500VDC system.

What is the typical switching frequency for this module in a solar application?

In high-power solar inverters, switching frequencies are typically kept low, between 2kHz and 8kHz, to minimize switching losses and maximize efficiency. This module is optimized for performance in that range.
