Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended gate driver voltage for this module?

For optimal performance and to ensure full saturation, a gate voltage of +15V (Vge) is recommended. The turn-off voltage should be between -8V and -15V.

Can this module be paralleled?

Yes, the GD100HFL120C1S has a positive temperature coefficient, which aids in current sharing when paralleling modules. However, careful PCB layout and gate driver design are critical for success. Contact our FAEs for application support.
