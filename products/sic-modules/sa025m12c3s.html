Frequently Asked Questions

Why use SiC instead of IGBT for an EV inverter?

SiC modules offer significantly lower switching losses, allowing for higher efficiency (which translates to longer vehicle range) and higher operating frequencies (which allows for a smaller, lighter, and more power-dense inverter).

What are the gate drive requirements for this SiC module?

SiC MOSFETs require a precise gate drive. We recommend a +18V to +20V turn-on voltage and a negative turn-off voltage (typically -3V to -5V) to ensure fast, clean switching and prevent parasitic turn-on. Contact our FAEs for a reference gate driver circuit.
