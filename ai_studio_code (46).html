FAE Application Insights

The GD400FFT170C1S is specifically designed for 3-level Neutral-Point Clamped (NPC) inverters. This topology is extremely popular in solar and UPS applications because it reduces the voltage stress on the switching devices and improves output waveform quality. This module simplifies the design of such systems by integrating the necessary IGBTs and diodes into a single package.
