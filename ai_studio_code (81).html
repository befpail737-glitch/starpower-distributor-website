FAE Application Insights

The GD25FFT120C1S is specifically designed for 3-level Neutral-Point Clamped (NPC) inverters. This topology is highly efficient and produces a cleaner output waveform compared to standard 2-level inverters. This module is an excellent choice for the DC/AC stage of uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) and solar inverters operating on a 600-800VDC bus, as it effectively halves the voltage stress on the switching devices.
