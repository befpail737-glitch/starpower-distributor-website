FAE Application Insights

This high-power H-bridge module is designed for demanding single-phase applications. It is commonly used in high-power UPS systems, industrial induction heating equipment, and as the core of a phase-shifted full-bridge (PSFB) DC/DC converter for applications like welding and battery charging. The integrated topology ensures matched switching characteristics.
