FAE Application Insights

The 'L1S' package is a low-profile housing designed for applications with height restrictions. The GD150HFL120L1S delivers high power in a flat package, making it ideal for compact inverter designs, such as those found in material handling vehicles (e.g., forklifts), and for creating blade-style modular UPS and servo systems where power density is the primary design driver.
